FAQ

Registration

  1. Obtaining a ticket

    You can obtain your ticket at Eventbrite droidconPL2017 registration website .

  2. Ticket prices
    • Early Birds available from 4th Sep 2017 to 29th Sep 2017 10:00AM (or until sold out) for 249PLN* (around 60€ **)
    • Standards available from 29th Sep 2017 to 20th Nov 2017 10:00AM (or until sold out) for 399PLN* (around 95€ **)
    • Late Birds available from 20th Nov 2017 to 2 Dec 2017 (or until sold out) for 549PLN* (around 130€ **)

      • *Prices do not include VAT and Eventbrite fees. **Dependable on current exchange rate.

  3. Tickets include
    • Entrance to all talks during both day of the conference.
    • Lunch and coffee breaks.
    • Swag bag including a droidcon Kraków 2017 branded T-shirt.
  4. Reimbursement policy

    Please keep in mind that we do not provide reimbursement for tickets purchased for droidcon Kraków 2017. However, there is the possibility to rebook your ticket to another person. In that case, please contact us at contact+rebook@droidcon.pl for further information.

General information

  1. Date, time and place

    droidcon Kraków 2017 will be held on 1st-2nd December 2017 at WPiA Jagiellonian University, Krupnicza 33a, 31-123 Kraków, Poland. MAP

  2. Language of the conference

    The conference will be held entirely in English including all of the talks, welcome and closing speeches.

  3. Code of Conduct

    As an attendee, speaker, sponsor or partner you are obligated to respect the Code of Conduct of the conference under the penalty of removal from conference without the right to reimbursement of the ticket.

Call For Papers & Agenda

  1. Becoming a Speaker

    To become a speaker at droidcon Kraków 2017 you have to fill the Call For Papers form and wait for our reply. Usually it takes around two weeks for us to get back to you with a decision. If we miss this date please be considerate that we are working hard on choosing the best lineup and it might take some more time sometimes.

  2. Talk and workshop duration
    • Talk slots are 30 or 45 minutes long.
    • Workshop slots are up to 2 hours, between 2 and 4 hours and more than 4 hours long.
  3. Speaker reimbursements

    If there is a need we can organize an accommodation for a Speaker for the conference duration. We don’t usually cover travel expenses. However, there is a possibility for your company to become a Travel Partner when travel expenses are covered from their end. Contact us for more details about this partnership contact@droidcon.pl .
