Call For Papers & Agenda

  1. Becoming a Speaker

    To become a speaker at droidcon Kraków 2017 you have to fill the Call For Papers form and wait for our reply. Usually it takes around two weeks for us to get back to you with a decision. If we miss this date please be considerate that we are working hard on choosing the best lineup and it might take some more time sometimes.

  2. Talk and workshop duration
    • Talk slots are 30 or 45 minutes long.
    • Workshop slots are up to 2 hours, between 2 and 4 hours and more than 4 hours long.
  3. Speaker reimbursements

    If there is a need we can organize an accommodation for a Speaker for the conference duration. We don’t usually cover travel expenses. However, there is a possibility for your company to become a Travel Partner when travel expenses are covered from their end. Contact us for more details about this partnership contact@droidcon.pl .

Barcamp sessions

  1. The idea

    General idea of Barcamp sessions is to give a chance to speak on the stage for those who didn’t get to the agenda through Call4Paper voting or didn’t have the opportunity yet to become a speaker before.

  2. How to do it

    Send us an email to contact@droidcon.pl with your full name, a title and short description (3-4 sentences) the talk. Make sure to put “Barcamp session” in email title.
    During the conference we will tell you to come to the stage and quickly present it in few words in front of the audience. After you do, we ask them to show us if they like it or not. When they clap their hands loudly - hurray! You can present topic within one of the Barcamp slots.

  3. Barcamp slot length

    Slots are 20 minutes long and there is no time for QA.

    Don't be shy and try! :)
