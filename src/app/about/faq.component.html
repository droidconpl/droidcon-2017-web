FAQ

Registration

  1. How do I get a ticket to droidcon Kraków 2017?

    You can obtain your ticket at Eventbrite droidconPL2017 registration website .

  2. What are ticket prices in 2017?
    • Early Bird available from 4th Sep 2017 to 29th Sep 2017 10:00AM (or until sold out) for 249 PLN* (around 60 € **)
    • Standard available from 29th Sep 2017 to 20th Nov 2017 10:00AM (or until sold out) for 399 PLN* (around 95 € **)
    • Late Bird available from 20th Nov 2017 to 2 Dec 2017 (or until sold out) for 549 PLN* (around 130 € **)

      • *Prices do not include VAT and Eventbrite fees. **Dependable on current exchange rate.

  3. What is included in the ticket price?
    • Entrance to all talks during both day of the conference.
    • Lunch and coffee breaks.
    • Swag bag including a droidcon Kraków 2017 branded T-shirt.
  4. Reimbursement policy

    Please keep in mind that we do not provide reimbursement for tickets purchased for droidcon Kraków 2017. However, there is the possibility to rebook your ticket to another person. In that case, please contact us at contact+rebook@droidcon.pl for further information.

General information

  1. Date, time and place

    droidcon Kraków 2017 will be held on 1-2 December 2017 at WPiA Jagiellonian University, Krupnicza 33a, 31-123 Kraków, Poland.

  2. Language of the conference

    The conference will be held entirely in English including all of the talks, welcome and closing speeches.

  3. Code of Conduct

    Each attendee, including speakers, sponsors, partners and attendees, is obligated to respect the Code of Conduct of the conference.
